02 — About

A designer in the making
curious, grounded, and growing.

My path into UI/UX Design hasn't been a straight line and I think that's what makes it honest. After completing my graduation and postgraduate studies, I spent time working in healthcare operations, where I learned the value of process, attention to detail, and clear communication.

Working closely with internal workflows and digital systems made me curious about why certain tools felt easy to use and others didn't. That curiosity led me to pursue a professional UI/UX Design certification, where I began learning user research, personas, empathy mapping, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Today, I'm focused on building a strong foundation in research driven design learning to listen to users before designing for them. I'm not here to claim years of experience I don't have. I'm here to grow, contribute, and design with empathy and care.
